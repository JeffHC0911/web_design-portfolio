About me

As a User Experience (UX) Designer, I specialize in creating intuitive and engaging digital experiences. With a strong foundation in graphic design, I focus on crafting visually appealing interfaces that prioritize usability and accessibility. My expertise spans wireframing, prototyping, and user research, ensuring that every design decision is backed by a deep understanding of user needs and behaviors.

I am proficient in industry-standard tools such as Figma, Adobe XD, and the Adobe Creative Suite, enabling me to deliver high-quality designs that align with brand identity and business objectives. My work bridges the gap between functionality and aesthetics, delivering solutions that not only look great but also enhance user satisfaction.
